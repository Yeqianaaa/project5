Under the Pandemic: Living in a Dfferent World

by Yeqian Li

This is a narrative mixed media installation project, which contains multiple small works, integrating various media forms such as clay, fabric and illustrations, showing the life of Chinese people during the COVID-19 in the past three years. I use artistic ways to let the audience understand this unusual experience, show the impact of the pandemic on people living in China, and resonate with people who have survived the COVID-19.
